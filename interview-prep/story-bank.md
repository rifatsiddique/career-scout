# Interview Story Bank

Accumulated STAR+R stories. Block F in `modes/evaluate.md` appends new stories here
during every evaluation run. Run `interview-prep --bank-review` to consolidate duplicates
and sharpen weak entries.

## How it works

Each story uses STAR+R with a small header for searchability:
- **Themes** — what the story is about (drives Story Bank Mapping in interview-prep)
- **Status** — `active` is the default; `superseded` stories are kept for history but
  ignored during mapping; `draft` means the story needs the user to flesh it out
- **Best for** — literal question phrasings this story answers well
