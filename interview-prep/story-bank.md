# Interview Story Bank

Accumulated STAR+R stories across evaluations. This bank grows over time as the system
maps your experiences to different JD requirements. Stories can be reused across applications.

## Format

Each story uses STAR+R:
- **Situation:** Context and background
- **Task:** What you needed to accomplish
- **Action:** What you specifically did (your role, not the team's)
- **Result:** Quantified outcome
- **Reflection:** What you learned, what you'd do differently (signals seniority)
